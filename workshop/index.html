2025 Legal Interpretation and Moral Reasoning Workshop

Dates: Wednesday 5th to Friday 7th of November 2025

Location: Salón de Actos, Carmen de la Victoria, University of Granada, Spain. [How to get there]

This workshop brings together eleven speakers from philosophy, law, and cognitive science to explore the interplay between legal interpretation and moral reasoning. Attendees can expect a dynamic program of talks, discussions, and collaborative sessions.
