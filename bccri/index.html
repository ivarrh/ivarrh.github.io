Bioxphi Cross-Cultural Research Initiative

The BioXphi Cross-Cultural Research Initiative seeks to expand BioXphi research across diverse cultural contexts around the world. By conducting studies internationally, we want explore both cultural variations and shared patterns in cognition, building a richer understanding of how culture shapes our responses to bioethical questions.

Our previous cross-cultural efforts in experimental jurisprudence have yielded some notable outcomes: The Nature of Reasonableness??, Another one. We are now excited to apply this methodology to the field of BioXphi.
